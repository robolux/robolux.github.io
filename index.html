About Me

Hunter is a passionate engineer that is involved in every aspect of robotic development from conceptualization to creation. He is well versed in C++, Java, XML, ROS, C, and Python. He enjoys long modeling sessions in Solid Edge and SolidWorks creating complex robotic systems using his trusty SpaceMouse. When it comes to fabricating the designs, he is capable of using the latest manufacturing equipment to get the job done from CNC machines to 3D-printing. The smell of solder indicates another long electronic prototyping session at his lab bench. Then his favorite part is the culmination of each robotic component into a novel robotic design.
