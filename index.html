About Me

Hunter is a passionate engineer that is involved in every aspect of robotic development from conceptualization to creation. He is well versed in C++, Java, XML, ROS, C, and Python. He enjoys long modeling sessions in Solid Edge and SolidWorks creating complex robotic systems using his trusty SpaceMouse. When it comes to fabricating the designs, he is capable of using the latest manufacturing equipment to get the job done from CNC machines to 3D-printing. The smell of solder indicates another long electronic prototyping session at his lab bench. Then his favorite part is the culmination of each robotic component into a novel robotic design.

Personal Projects

I completed independent research on the development of a sub 7.5cm UAV. The research includes characterization of flight in low Reynolds number conditions and the subsequent design of a novel UAV. It involved the development of a custom flight controller, airframe, and tilt-rotor control methods.

The research project won 1st at ASEF, 2nd at NARSEF, and 2nd at ISWEEEP in the category of Engineering. It also won 1st at the Gorgas Scholarship Program, Boeing Best of Show and the NASA Award of Excellence at ASEF, and 9 other various special awards throughout the competitions, along with being invited to present at the Young Professional and Student Technical Poster Session for the AIAA Greater Huntsville Section.

I also enjoying coding and CADing on my own personal computer outfitted with all the nerd goodies.
